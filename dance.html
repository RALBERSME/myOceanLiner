Boston

As a modification of the Viennese waltz, the Boston originated in the USA around 1870 and became popular in Europe in 1913.
Its special feature is that it involves dancing two steps per three beats.

click this line to watch Boston Waltz 1909

Foxtrot

The foxtrot developed from the one- and two-step in the USA between 1910 and 1915. From this, the slow foxtrot and the quickstep emerged as variants from 1924 onwards. .

click this line to watch Foxtrot Finesse (1915) - Just the Dancing Bits

The Two-Step

The two-step is similar to the polka and is particularly popular in the southern states of the USA. It is danced to fast Cajun music. Its figures will later find their way into the disco fox. .

(by Irving Berlin -1915)

I know a millionaire, Who's burdened down with care, A load is on his mind.
He's thinking of the day When he must pass away And leave his wealth behind.
I haven't any gold To leave when I grow old, Somehow it passed me by.

I'll leave the moon above to those in love,

When I leave the world behind.

To ev'ry broken heart With sorrow torn apart, I'll leave the love of God.
