Lifeboats

From the studies done after the Titanic, recommendations were made to have enough lifeboats for all. We have enough lifeboats on board.

Lifeboats

Yes, we had enough lifeboats on board.
However see next card concerning the devices to launch lifeboats.

Devices to launch lifeboats

From the studies done after the Titanic, recommendations were made to use devices to help launch lifeboats while a boat was listing.

Devices to launch lifeboats

We had not promised this because we had not implemented this important insight. So it did not matter that we had enough lifeboats, because the lack of equipment meant that most of them could not be used.

Lifejackets

From the studies done after the Titanic, recommendations were made to have enough lifejackets for all. We have enough lifejackets on board.

Lifejackets

Yes, we had 600 more than needed. But many passengers did not have one in their hands when the ship sank (more than 50%) and those who had one very often did not know how to use it and then put them on incorrectly, so that their effect was useless.
